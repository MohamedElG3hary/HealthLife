what is sleep?

It is a natural state of the body and mind, but it is not specifically present in motor and physical activity.

Benefits of sleep

  • Improve memory and concentration.
  • Strengthening the immune system.
  • Helping with emotional and psychological balance.
  • Improving physical performance.

Sleep stages

Sleep goes through four main stages:

  1. Drowsiness stage.
  2. The stage of light sleep.
  3. Deep sleep stage.
  4. Rapid eye movement (REM) phase.

Tips for good sleep

  • Maintain a regular sleep schedule.
  • Avoid consuming caffeine before bed.
  • Create a comfortable, dark sleeping environment.
  • Exercise regularly.
