Components of Fitness

  • Cardiovascular Endurance: The ability of your heart, lungs, and circulatory system to supply oxygen and nutrients efficiently during prolonged physical activity. Examples: Running, cycling, swimming.
  • Muscular Strength: The ability of a muscle or group of muscles to exert force. Examples: Weightlifting, resistance exercises.
  • Muscular Endurance: The ability of muscles to continue to perform without fatigue. Examples: Planking, bodyweight exercises.

Benefits of Regular Exercise

  1. Improves Mental Health: Reduces symptoms of depression and anxiety.
  2. Physical Health: Lowers the risk of chronic diseases such as heart disease, diabetes, and obesity.
  3. Sleep Quality: Regular physical activity helps improve sleep patterns.
  4. Increases Longevity: Regular exercisers tend to live longer, healthier lives.
